About CERI
Established in 2012, the Centre for Economic Research and Intergovernmental Co-operation is committed to enhancing global quality of life by refining policies across local, national, and international dimensions. The organization conducts conscientious research, encourages policy dialogue, and formulates strategic frameworks that promote informed decision-making.
