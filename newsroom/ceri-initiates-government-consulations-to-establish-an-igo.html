CERI initiates government consultations to establish an IGO
Press release • 16 March 2025

Summary: CERI has begun consultations with officials in France, the United States, and Colombia on establishing CERI as an intergovernmental organization focused on economic policy in education.

Status:

  • Outreach to ministries responsible for education, economy, and foreign affairs is underway.
  • CERI seeks two or more founding state sponsors to negotiate a founding statute and a host-state headquarters agreement.

Next steps:

  • Convening an exploratory virtual meeting with interested governments in Q1 2026.
  • Publishing a short governance note and draft statute for comment.

Media: press@ceri.org

Working with governments and institutions across multiple countries, CERI is a global policy forum that advances evidence-based cooperation to strengthen economic, social, educational, and health outcomes while upholding individual liberty and human dignity.
