Education financing efficiency review
Press release • 04 August 2025

Summary: CERI will analyze spending efficiency and equity in upper-secondary and vocational tracks to inform budget decisions.

Objectives:

  • Map spending to outcomes (completion, employment, NEET rates).
  • Identify 3–5 high-yield reallocations within existing budgets.
  • Stakeholder workshop with regional authorities.

Deliverables:

  • Efficiency brief with costed scenarios.
  • Dashboard linking spend to outcomes across communes.

Timeline:

Q2–Q4 2026

Partner eligibility:

Académies, regional councils, or ministries seeking expenditure-to-outcome alignment.

Media: press@ceri.org

Working with governments and institutions across multiple countries, CERI is a global policy forum that advances evidence-based cooperation to strengthen economic, social, educational, and health outcomes while upholding individual liberty and human dignity.
