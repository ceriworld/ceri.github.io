Education-to-workforce mobility dashboard
Press release • 22 September 2025

Summary: CERI will prototype a regional dashboard connecting education pathways to earnings and employment.

Objectives:

  • Track transitions from high school/college to jobs and wages.
  • Identify bottlenecks and high-ROI pathways (CTE, apprenticeships).

Deliverables:

  • Public dashboard (beta) and technical documentation.
  • Policy recommendations for funding and employer partnerships.
  • Briefing for state agencies and workforce boards.

Timeline:

Q3 2026–Q1 2027

Partner eligibility:

State education/workforce agencies, large districts, community colleges.

Media: press@ceri.org

Working with governments and institutions across multiple countries, CERI is a global policy forum that advances evidence-based cooperation to strengthen economic, social, educational, and health outcomes while upholding individual liberty and human dignity.
