Critical literacy in bilingual classrooms
Press release • 18 July 2025

Summary: CERI and a bilingual school network will co-design modules that affirm identity and strengthen critique in reading and writing.

Objectives:

  • 6-module bilingual curriculum pack.
  • Rapid evaluation report (90 days).
  • Policy note for Secretarías de Educación.

Deliverables:

  • Increase motivation and text comprehension in Spanish/English.
  • Train teachers in discussion-based methods.

Timeline:

Q2–Q4 2026

Partner eligibility:

Bilingual schools or public–private partnerships in Bogotá/Soacha.

Media: press@ceri.org

Working with governments and institutions across multiple countries, CERI is a global policy forum that advances evidence-based cooperation to strengthen economic, social, educational, and health outcomes while upholding individual liberty and human dignity.
